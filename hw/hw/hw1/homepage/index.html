About Daniel

Daniel Figgis Gonzalez was born September 27, 2001 in Miami, Florida. He now lives in Boston, Massachusetts and is attending the MIT studying Humanities and Engineering with foci in Comparative Media Studies and Computer Science. Currently working with the Poetic Justice Group at the MIT Media Lab, he seeks to apply technical marvels to humanitarian goals.

When not working, Daniel can be found volunteering his time at DormCon or running FRED DESK at East Campus. He shares two cats with his partner, Val, whose names are Dusty and Eda. He has a preference for Eda.
